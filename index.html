About

Built on a simple belief

I'm the founder of AsRaven — a one-person studio focused on Android development and technical solutions. I take on a limited number of projects at a time, and I'm deliberate about which ones those are.

I've seen what happens when teams try to do too much at once. Mediocre products ship, corners get cut, and the work suffers. I've chosen a different path: fewer commitments, deeper focus, and outcomes that I'm actually proud of.
